Remembering Helena


Commemorative Bracelet made from the contents of my mother-in-law's dressing table drawer for her daughter - three singleton earrings, a thimble, and two coins no longer in circulation.

Final Journey


Part of a design challenge, the boat and urns are fold-formed copper, the mast is a sterling silver tube etched in silver nitrate. The sail is sterling silver roller-printed using a brass plate etched from an original design. The lines holding the mast upright are made from a pipe cleaner. Also included in the construction are copper and sterling silver wire, copper rivets, and fine silver mesh.


This boat was made to commemorate the passing of eight close family members within a three and one half year period. The scrolls in the little urns each contain the name of one of our loved ones.
